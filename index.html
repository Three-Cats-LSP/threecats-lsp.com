Diver's Toolkit

Precision tools for the modern scuba diver — built by divers, for divers.

LSP D-Planner

A dive planning app for recreational and technical divers. Covers PADI RDP tables, Bühlmann ZH-L16C decompression with Gradient Factors, and VPM-B bubble model. Includes gas management, CNS O₂ tracking, nitrox/trimix tools, and contingency planning — all in one offline web app.

Open App

T-Viewer

Companion app for LSP D-Planner. Open, read, and share the dive plan text and PDF files exported by LSP D-Planner directly on your Android device. Supports syntax highlighting for deco schedules, depth data, warnings, and gas labels.

Open App

Get In Water

Pack your dive trip. Customizable master gear template, per-trip checklists, and TXT/PDF export for printing. Companion to LSP D-Planner.

About Three Cats LSP

Three Cats LSP is an independent home project creating 3D-printed diving gear gadgets for scuba divers, and the developer of LSP D-Planner — a deco schedule app packed with tools for your underwater adventures. Built by people for people who know what matters underwater.
